Internship

It's no secret that internships are one of the best practices to gain a full-time job offer. Growing as a high-performing intern is an exotic way to progress your employment prospects. Internships can be a great kick start to get your foot in the door with a desirable company. Internship at Saharsh Code Craft Labs gives you on-the-job experience, helps you learn whether you and the industry working for are a good match, providing you with valuable references and connections references. Our interns are usually exposed to a wide variety of tasks and responsibilities, allowing the intern to present their strengths by working on projects for various managers on different parts of the organizations. Explore internship opportunities with Saharsh Code Craft Labs across various industries and job functions.


Why Internship at Saharsh Code Craft Labs

  • In India over 60% of engineering students are being unemployed due to a significant skill gap.
  • Educational institutions train millions of youngsters, but corporates often complain that they do not get the necessary skill and talent required for a job.
  • As Domain experts, we are helping to fill the skill gap for engineering students and corporates by conducting training and internship programs in our premises on current projects we are handling.
  • Since we are situated in the core industrial area in Hyderabad, manufacturing industries and industrial experts are associated with us to help the students develop their skills more practically.

Benefits to the Students

  • We have a team of engineers who are experts in industries for more than 10 years in the respective field; students will be working under them as a team.
  • Since we handle the projects from conceptual to manufacturing level, students can gain enormous knowledge on various stages, how the product will be developed in industries.
  • We always encourage students to involve in creating new ideas and take them into consideration to develop the concept.
  • Internship or course completion certificates will be provided for their future endeavors.
